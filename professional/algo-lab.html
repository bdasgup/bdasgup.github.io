History
The lab has been around since 2004. Bulk of its initial funding was provided by NSF (CAREER) grant IIS-0346973 over a five year period. Other past supporting NSF grants include DBI-0543365, CCR-0296041, CNS-020679, CCF-0208749, IIS-1160995, DBI-1062328, CCF-1216096 and IIS-1814931.

Location
Room 1228B SEL (West)

Mission
Our goal is to understand fundamental algorithmic complexity of problems that arise in several different interdisciplinary areas such as biology, physics, mathematics, control theory, finance and economics. We believe in free dissemination of scientific information.

For publications from our lab, see the paper repository of the lab.

For softwares, see both the paper repository and the software repository of the lab.
